BioSite
For this project, we were supposed to create a biography website about someone we knew personally in our lives. I decided on my mother, and designed a site that expressed who she was, what she did for a living, what she's achieved in her life, and the hobbies she enjoys doing on a day-to-day basis. You can check out this project here: BioSite
